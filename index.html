While riding our bikes to brunch one Sunday afternoon (without helmets), Christian was hit by a car. This was a major wake-up call and made us ask ourselves why don’t we wear helmets? To put it simply, they’re bulky, hot, and annoying to carrying around. We realized these reasons were at the expense of our safety and possibly even our lives. As experts in Product Development and Design, we decided to take matters into our own hands. Enter FEND. A bicycle helmet we would actually want to wear without compromising our core values.

Christian Von Heifner with over ten years of industrial design and mechanical engineering experience has led projects with global clients such as Panasonic and Unilever. Most recently he launched HERO health as CoFounder and Head of Design and Mechanical Engineering.

Sujene Kong with over ten years of experience in Retail Merchandising has managed the Accessories divisions of global luxury brands such as Saint Laurent, Jimmy Choo and Diesel. Most recently she oversaw the merchandising strategy for the Accessories division at Burberry Americas.
